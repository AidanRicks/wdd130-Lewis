Overview

Purpose

Our Firearm & Concealed Carry Resource website is dedicated to providing reliable, well-researched information on responsible gun ownership, concealed carry gear, and best safety practices. We aim to equip individuals with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about firearms and self-defense.

Audience

Our primary audience includes concealed carry permit holders, firearm enthusiasts, and individuals seeking guidance on responsible gun ownership. Whether they are new to concealed carry or experienced firearm users looking for gear recommendations and safety tips, our content is designed to be accessible, informative, and practical.

Firearm Safety & Training

1. Treat every firearm as if it is loaded - Always assume a gun is loaded, even if you believe it isn't. 2. Never point the muzzle at anything you are not willing to destroy - Always keep the gun pointed in a safe direction, away from people and animals. 3. Keep your finger off the trigger until you are ready to shoot - Keep your finger outside the trigger guard when not actively engaging a target. 4. Be sure of your target and what is beyond it - Identify your target clearly and make sure there is nothing behind it that could be accidentally hit. 5. Store firearms securely - When not in use, ensure firearms are stored in a safe, secure location, out of reach of unauthorized persons. 6. Know how to properly operate your firearm - Familiarize yourself with the firearm's controls and ensure you understand how it works. 7. Use the correct ammunition - Always use the ammunition specified by the manufacturer for your firearm. 8. Wear proper safety gear - Always wear eye and ear protection when shooting.

Holster and Gun Reviews

The Sig Sauer P365 is a standout in the concealed carry world, offering impressive capacity in a tiny, easily concealable package. With a 10+1 round capacity and excellent accuracy for its size, it's a top choice for those seeking a small, reliable everyday carry handgun.

The M&P Shield is known for its slim, lightweight design, making it one of the best choices for concealed carry. With a comfortable grip, smooth trigger, and excellent reliability, it's a solid option for those looking for a no-fuss carry gun.

The Glock 19 is a reliable, versatile handgun that's a favorite among both law enforcement and civilians. Its compact size makes it ideal for concealed carry, while its accuracy and 15-round magazine provide a balance of capacity and performance.

IWB holsters are a top choice for concealed carry due to their excellent concealment and comfort, as they sit discreetly inside the waistband. Adjustable cant and ride height options allow for a customized fit, making it easy to find the ideal carry position.

OWB holsters are known for offering easy access to your firearm and comfort for long periods of wear, though they can be harder to conceal under tighter clothing. They are ideal for those who prioritize comfort and prefer to carry in a more accessible location.

Belly band holsters are a versatile and comfortable option, as they allow for concealed carry around the waist or torso. They are especially useful for those who need to carry in various positions or who wear more fitted clothing, offering flexibility and the ability to carry multiple firearms or accessories discreetly.
