Holster and Gun Reviews

Sig Sauer P365

The Sig Sauer P365 is a standout in the concealed carry world, offering impressive capacity in a tiny, easily concealable package. With a 10+1 round capacity and excellent accuracy for its size, it's a top choice for those seeking a small, reliable everyday carry handgun.

M&P Shield

The M&P Shield is known for its slim, lightweight design, making it one of the best choices for concealed carry. With a comfortable grip, smooth trigger, and excellent reliability, it's a solid option for those looking for a no-fuss carry gun.

Glock 19

The Glock 19 is a reliable, versatile handgun that's a favorite among both law enforcement and civilians. Its compact size makes it ideal for concealed carry, while its accuracy and 15-round magazine provide a balance of capacity and performance.

IWB Holsters

IWB holsters are a top choice for concealed carry due to their excellent concealment and comfort, as they sit discreetly inside the waistband. Adjustable cant and ride height options allow for a customized fit, making it easy to find the ideal carry position.

OWB Holsters

OWB holsters are known for offering easy access to your firearm and comfort for long periods of wear, though they can be harder to conceal under tighter clothing. They are ideal for those who prioritize comfort and prefer to carry in a more accessible location.

Belly Band Holsters

Belly band holsters are a versatile and comfortable option, as they allow for concealed carry around the waist or torso. They are especially useful for those who need to carry in various positions or who wear more fitted clothing, offering flexibility and the ability to carry multiple firearms or accessories discreetly.
